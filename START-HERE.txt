Welcome! This is a small tool that researches any company on the live web and
gives you a clean, sourced profile to help with your job search — products, news,
controversies, culture, open roles, and a tailored "is this a good fit for me?"
read based on the background you provide.

You do NOT need to know how to code to use it.

HOW TO SET UP YOUR OWN COPY
---------------------------
Open the file named "README.md" and follow the section titled
"Make it your own". It walks you through three short steps, all in your web
browser (about 15 minutes the first time):

  1. Get your own Anthropic API key (this is what pays for the research).
  2. Put these files into your own free GitHub account.
  3. Put it online with Vercel and type in your name + a short bio.

That's it. Everything personal — your key, your name, your background — is
something you type into a simple web form. None of it is in the files.

Tip: you can read README.md nicely formatted by opening this folder on
github.com after step 2, or in any text editor.
