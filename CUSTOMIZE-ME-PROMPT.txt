HOW TO USE THIS FILE
--------------------
This makes writing your "ABOUT_ME" easy. Copy everything below the dashed line
and paste it into any AI chat (claude.ai or ChatGPT). It will interview you,
one question at a time, and hand back the exact text to paste into Vercel
during Step 3 of the setup guide.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I'm setting up a personal "company profiler" tool for my job search. It
researches a company and ends with a "Fit & Angle" section tailored to me. To
personalize it, I need to fill in two settings:

1. NEXT_PUBLIC_OWNER_NAME — just my first name.
2. ABOUT_ME — a short, well-organized summary of my professional background
   and what I'm looking for.

Please interview me to build these. Ask me ONE question at a time, wait for my
answer, and keep it conversational. Cover at least:

- My first name.
- My current or most recent job title and level (and roughly how many years
  of experience I have).
- A quick sketch of my career so far (main employers or industries, in order).
- What I'm best known for professionally — my strengths and specialties.
- The kind of role I'm looking for next (title, level, full-time or other).
- My field or function (e.g. marketing, finance, engineering, HR).
- What makes a company a GOOD fit for me (size, stage, industry, culture,
  values — whatever matters to me).
- Any deal-breakers or things that make a company a BAD fit.
- My location and whether I want remote, hybrid, or in-office.

If any of my answers are vague, ask one short follow-up to make them concrete.

When you have everything, output exactly two things and nothing else:

1. A line that says: NEXT_PUBLIC_OWNER_NAME = <my first name>
2. A block labeled "ABOUT_ME" containing 5-8 plain-language bullet points
   summarizing me, written in the first person, ready to paste as one value.
   Don't use any special formatting characters — just simple dashes and text.
